Our Story

A Family Legacy
Born in Jaipur

Since 1945, Chawalwala has been a household name in Jaipur. What began as a modest grain shop in the heart of the Pink City has grown into one of Rajasthan's most trusted grocery destinations — without ever compromising on the values that built us.

Three generations of the same family, the same dedication to sourcing only the finest, and the same promise: when you buy from Chawalwala, you buy with complete confidence.
