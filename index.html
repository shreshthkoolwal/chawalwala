Our Story

A Family Legacy
Born in Jaipur

Since 1945, Chawalwala has been a household name in Jaipur. What began as a modest grain shop in the heart of the Pink City has grown into one of Rajasthan's most trusted grocery destinations.

Three generations of the same family, the same dedication to sourcing only the finest, and the same promise: when you buy from Chawalwala, you buy with complete confidence.
